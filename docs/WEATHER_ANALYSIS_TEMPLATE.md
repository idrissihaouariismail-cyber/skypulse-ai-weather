# Weather Analysis Template

## Format Guidelines

**Structure:**
1. **Short Title** (3-5 words)
2. **Brief Summary** (1-2 sentences)
3. **Practical Recommendation** (action-oriented)

**Tone:**
- Human-friendly
- Clear and concise
- Action-oriented
- No technical jargon
- Decision-focused

---

## Examples

### Example 1: Rainy Day

**Light Rain Expected**
Today will bring scattered showers throughout the afternoon. The rain will be light but persistent, so plan accordingly.
**Recommendation:** Carry an umbrella if heading out, and consider indoor activities for the afternoon.

---

### Example 2: Hot Weather

**Very Hot Day Ahead**
Temperatures will reach 38°C with high humidity, making it feel even warmer. The heat will peak between 2-5 PM.
**Recommendation:** Stay indoors during peak hours, drink plenty of water, and avoid strenuous outdoor activities.

---

### Example 3: Windy Conditions

**Strong Winds Today**
Gusty winds up to 45 km/h are expected, especially in open areas. The wind will be strongest in the afternoon.
**Recommendation:** Secure outdoor items, drive carefully on highways, and avoid outdoor activities near trees or structures.

---

### Example 4: Clear and Pleasant

**Perfect Weather Today**
Sunny skies with comfortable temperatures around 24°C and light breeze. Ideal conditions for outdoor activities.
**Recommendation:** Great day for outdoor plans! Perfect weather for a walk, picnic, or any outdoor activities you have planned.

---

### Example 5: Cold Snap

**Cold Morning Ahead**
Temperatures will drop to 5°C early morning with light frost possible. It will warm up to 12°C by afternoon.
**Recommendation:** Dress in layers for the morning, and wait until afternoon for outdoor activities if possible.

---

### Example 6: Storm Warning

**Thunderstorms Expected**
Heavy rain and thunderstorms are likely this evening, with potential for strong winds and lightning.
**Recommendation:** Stay indoors this evening. If you must go out, avoid open areas and seek shelter immediately if storms approach.

---

### Example 7: Foggy Conditions

**Dense Fog This Morning**
Visibility will be reduced to less than 200 meters until mid-morning. The fog will clear by 10 AM.
**Recommendation:** Allow extra travel time this morning. Drive slowly with low beams, and avoid unnecessary trips until visibility improves.

---

### Example 8: Air Quality Alert

**Poor Air Quality Today**
Air quality index is elevated due to pollution and weather conditions. Sensitive groups may experience discomfort.
**Recommendation:** Limit outdoor activities, especially if you have respiratory issues. Keep windows closed and use air purifiers if available.

---

## Template Structure

```
**TITLE** (3-5 words describing main condition)

SUMMARY (1-2 sentences explaining what's happening)

**Recommendation:** ACTION-ORIENTED ADVICE (what the user should do)
```

---

## Key Principles

1. **Be Specific:** Mention times, temperatures, or conditions when relevant
2. **Be Helpful:** Focus on what the user can do, not just what's happening
3. **Be Concise:** Keep it short - users want quick insights
4. **Be Clear:** Avoid weather jargon (e.g., "low pressure system" → "stormy weather")
5. **Be Actionable:** Always end with a clear recommendation

---

## Weather Condition Categories

### Temperature Extremes
- Very Hot → Stay cool, hydrate, avoid peak hours
- Very Cold → Dress warmly, protect exposed skin, limit time outside

### Precipitation
- Light Rain → Carry umbrella, minor inconvenience
- Heavy Rain → Stay indoors, avoid travel
- Snow → Drive carefully, dress warmly

### Wind
- Light Breeze → Pleasant conditions
- Strong Winds → Secure items, drive carefully
- Very Strong → Stay indoors, avoid outdoor activities

### Visibility
- Clear → Normal activities
- Fog → Drive carefully, allow extra time
- Haze → Limit outdoor activities if sensitive

### Air Quality
- Good → Normal activities
- Moderate → Sensitive groups take caution
- Poor → Limit outdoor activities

## Tone Examples

❌ **Too Technical:**
"Low pressure system moving in from the west will bring convective precipitation with potential for isolated thunderstorms."

✅ **Human-Friendly:**
"Rain and thunderstorms are expected this afternoon. Stay indoors if possible."

---

❌ **Too Vague:**
"The weather will be variable today."

✅ **Specific:**
"Sunny morning, but rain will arrive by 3 PM. Plan outdoor activities for the morning."

---

❌ **No Action:**
"Temperatures will be very high today."

✅ **Action-Oriented:**
"Very hot day ahead. Stay hydrated and avoid outdoor activities during peak heat hours (2-5 PM)."
